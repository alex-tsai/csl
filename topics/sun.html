Are powered by the Sun

Plants, through photosynthesis, convert sunlight into energy they can use. The sun warms, lights and powers the CSL.

Green Roof

The solar panels facing Panther Hollow and on the roof of the adjacent building generate more electricity than the CSL uses.

Interior

A lot of high performance windows allow ample daylight to permeate CSL interior, resulting in artificial lights being off nearly 85% of the time.

Landscape

Projections extending from CSL windows on the inside and outside both block excess summer sun so it stays cool inside and reflect light into the interior, lighting the office area.
